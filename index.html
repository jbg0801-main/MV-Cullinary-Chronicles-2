and here are some of my favourite foods:

Pasta

Pasta is a plain food coming in many different variants, great for covering in all sorts of toppings, sauces, or combinations of the two. I have an unhealthy relationship with Pasta, living and dying by my unending consumption of it. I'm a fan of adding cheese, tomato sauce, tuna, or a combination of the three.

Pizza

Pizza is a classic, it's hard to hate. With endless possibilities found in toppings, and a simple base to build off, there's pizza for everyone (unless you put pineapples on it you monster, then there's no pizza for you, only Hellmo.) personally, I'm partial to a plain Margherita with Tuna on top.

Donuts

Donuts are great as an occasional treat, and come in many flavours, shapes and sizes, which is most of what makes them appealing to me. I'm particularly partial to jam-filled donuts, as well as basically any variant of donuts you can get at Lidl
